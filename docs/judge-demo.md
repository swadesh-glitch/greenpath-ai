# Judge Demo Guide

This document provides a structured walkthrough script and checklist for showing the GreenPath AI app during a presentation or demo.

## Demo Flow

### 1. The Hook (Landing Page)
- Highlight the problem statement: Generic carbon calculators are boring.
- Introduce GreenPath AI's personalized, interactive ecosystem.
- Point out the gorgeous visual aesthetics and micro-animations.

### 2. Personalization (Onboarding & Identity)
- Walk through the interactive assessment questionnaire.
- Show the final screen generating a specific **Climate Identity** (e.g., Conscious Commuter or Green Foodie) with custom bonuses.

### 3. Actionable Tasks (Missions Hub)
- Navigate to the Missions page.
- Show daily checklist and complete a task (demonstrating point increases, CO2 savings calculation, and completion animations).
- Complete a series of tasks to trigger a streak update.

### 4. Interactive Growth (Carbon Garden)
- Navigate to the Carbon Garden.
- Show the visual garden representation.
- Observe how planting new vegetation and leveling up the garden visually represents the carbon saved in the Missions hub.

### 5. Profile & Carbon History (Profile & Climate Story)
- View the overall user achievements, levels, and total carbon saved.
- Check the visual timeline of actions taken since onboarding.
